The CyberDeck project is committed to a professional and safe environment for all participants.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation harassment-free for everyone, regardless of:

- age;
- body size;
- visible or invisible disability;
- ethnicity;
- gender identity and expression;
- experience level;
- nationality;
- personal appearance;
- race;
- religion;
- sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Respectful and constructive communication.
- Fact-based, verifiable technical discussion.
- Openness to feedback without personal attacks.
- Transparent discussions around architecture, quality, and security.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Insults, intimidation, threats, or harassment.
- Hate speech or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Deliberate disruption of review or collaboration workflows.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in:

- Issues, Pull Requests, and Discussions;
- code review and commit communication;
- public spaces where a participant represents the project.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer:

- GitHub: <https://github.com/Overl1te>
- Issues: <https://github.com/Overl1te/CyberDeck/issues>

Please include:

- what happened;
- where and when it happened;
- links/screenshots/logs when available.

Reports are reviewed promptly and handled with discretion where possible.

## Enforcement

Depending on severity and repetition, maintainers may apply:

1. Correction.
2. Warning and interaction limits.
3. Temporary ban.
4. Permanent ban.
